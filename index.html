Ashton's Ticky Tacky Toes

A slightly comedic take on the classic Tic Tac Toe game!

Find a friend and take turns placing toes and tacks on the game board below! Player One will start by placing a toe in any squuare of their choice, followed by Player Two placing a tack on any square of their choice. The game will continue in this fashion until one player acheives three of their symbols in a row, or until the board is full.

In the event of a tie, please use the "Reset Gameboard" button located below!
